About Me

I have been a graduate student at the University of Iowa for almost two years. My area of research concerns stochastic differential equations (SDEs). These are differential equations which have a probabilistic term added to them, which gives these types of equations a sense of randomness. Consequently, we can use them to model phenomena that exhibit background noise, such as signals or large collections of data.
