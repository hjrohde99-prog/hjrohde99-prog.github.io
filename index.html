About Me

I have been a graduate student in the University of Iowa's Applied Mathematical and Computational Sciences (AMCS) Program for almost two years. My area of research concerns stochastic differential equations (SDEs). These are differential equations which have a probabilistic term added to them, which gives these types of equations a sense of randomness. Consequently, we can use them to model phenomena that exhibit background noise, such as signals or large collections of data.
